about me

Im a motivated 3D artist with a passion for environments. I have completed individual projects which include sci-fi themed environments, as well as more natural themed enviroements. I have also worked with other individuals on projects for games whilst completing my studies at Staffordshire University. One of my main hobbies is photography which i use a lot when working on projects either for inspiration or gathering textures.
